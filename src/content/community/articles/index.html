NetBeans WorldTour
The NetBeans team announces their FREE worldTour! NetBeans enthusiasts like you travel to a dozen cities around the world each year to show you more demos (and less slides), so you can see NetBeans in action and exactly how it can increase your productivity. Register now in a city near you, it's free, and seriously the best way to meet fellow developers and talk shop.
Interviews With Engineers and Community Members
What's hot in NetBeansland? Read the latest about new technologies and the people who create them. What does James Gosling think about AJAX, and how does Tim Boudreau see the future of NetBeans? Read about plug-in module writers, RCP developers, and user interface designers who share their secrets and best practices with you. Listen to technology evangelists like Roman and Gregg Sporar who recorded a whole podcast. You too have a NetBeans story to tell? Well, don't hesitate, get in touch!
Shows, Competitions, and Events
Articles, reports, and information about past and future NetBeans events -- There are interesting events all over the world, find one near you! Apart from the yearly NetBeans WorldTour, you will also find out more about developer competitions (with cool prizes!), various international presentations and community awards. You'll find current upcoming events listed in the NetBeans calendar.
NetBeans Video Tutorials and Screencasts
Here you can find videos and demos which will give you a picture of cool features of the current NetBeans release. Download them, make some popcorn, invite your friends over -- it's like having your own NetBeans day in your livingroom. Often you will even find first peeks at bleeding-edge beta features here.
