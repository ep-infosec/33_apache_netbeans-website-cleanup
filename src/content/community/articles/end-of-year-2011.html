End of Year Message 2011

With the end of the year quickly approaching, the NetBeans Team would like to thank the NetBeans community for its support over the past year. This has been a milestone year for NetBeans where we've hit the 1,000,000 active user mark. We've also released NetBeans IDE 7.0 and NetBeans IDE 7.0.1, and underpinned the success of many other projects.

We are currently putting the finishing touches on our current release, NetBeans IDE 7.1. Given the holidays and festivities being near, we are going to hold off on releasing NetBeans IDE 7.1 until early next year, when it will have maximum impact.

Again, we thank all of you for your encouragement and support, and wish you and yours happy holidays and a great New Year.

The NetBeans Team
