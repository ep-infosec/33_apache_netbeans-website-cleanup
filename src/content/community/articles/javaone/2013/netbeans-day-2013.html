NetBeans Community Day at JavaOne 2013 Conference


Join the NetBeans team at NetBeans Community Day at JavaOne 2013 to celebrate 15 Years of NetBeans!

We'll share highlights of how NetBeans has evolved; what's new and noteworthy today, and explore what's up ahead for NetBeans IDE.

Various organizations and Java experts will also show how they are using NetBeans technology to deliver innovative solutions and applications.

Lastly, there'll be NetBeans anniversary gifts for attendees! Don't Miss NetBeans Community Day 2013!

Date: Sunday, September 22
Location: Moscone West, Hall 3024

Add your name to the NetBeans Day Guest List

NetBeans Community Day is free to registered JavaOne conference attendees. We also encourage you to preregister using your JavaOne Schedule Builder.
