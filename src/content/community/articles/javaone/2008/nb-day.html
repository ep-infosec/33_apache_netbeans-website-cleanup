NetBeans Day 2008 at the CommunityOne Conference



Monday, May 5, 2008
9:30 am - 6:00 pm
Moscone Center,
San Francisco, California

What Is It?


NetBeans Day at CommunityOne is FREE - you do not have to be registered for JavaOne to attend.

On Monday, May 5, the day before the 2008 JavaOne Conference, the NetBeans Team hosts a FREE companion event at the Moscone Center. Learn from the experts about what's new and what's coming: Swing Application Framework and Beans Binding, Editor, Profiler, Ruby, PHP, and the Visual Mobile Designer.

Highlights

  • Grab a lunch box and listen to the Java Posse (Dick Wall, Tor Norbye, Carl Quinn and Joe Nuxoll) record a podcast live on stage.
  • See live demos of NetBeans IDE 6.1 - Swing Application Framework and Beans Binding, Editor, Profiler, Ruby, and the Visual Mobile Designer.
  • Chat with the NetBeans Evangelism Team and ask them tough questions.
  • Meet fellow developers and share ideas.
  • Watch demos of cool applications built with the NetBeans IDE and the NetBeans Platform.
