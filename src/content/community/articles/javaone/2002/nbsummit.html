NetBeans Summit at JavaOne

We are putting together a NetBeans Summit Meeting. The Summit will start at 6pm and go for about two hours. You will have a chance to talk to the developers of NetBeans that you probably only know from the mailing lists or you can talk to companies building applications and plugins on NetBeans. You do not need to be attending JavaOne to attend this NetBeans meeting.

You can also meet us at the NetBeans booth at 5:40 and go to the conference room with us.

Logistics

When:
Wednesday, March 27th, 2002
Where:
The W Hotel (across the street from the Moscone Center in San Francisco, where JavaOne is)
181 Third Street
San Francisco, California 94103

Agenda

  • 18:00 - 18:20 General introduction and overview of NetBeans
  • 18:20 - 19:20 NetBeans Architecture overview
  • 19:20 - 19:40 Future Plans with NetBeans
  • 19:40 - 21:00 Panel Discussion and Brainstorming Sessions
