Come see us at JavaONE 2002

JavaOne is Sun Microsystem's worldwide developer conference. This year's JavaONE will be at the Moscone Center in San Francisco and will take place between March 25th and 29th. We would like to invite you to the following:
  • Special Event! The NetBeans Summit Meeting - If you'd like to meet the people behind NetBeans, register by sending an email to George Dvorak. The agenda and details are here. You do not need to be attending JavaOne to attend this event.
  • NetBeans IDE Pod - come see us! Located near the Sun Microsystems booth.
  • NetBeans Platform Booth - NetBeans in action! A number of the companies that have built applications on top of the NetBeans platform will be demoing their products here!
  • NetBeans module vendors - see demos of extensions to the NetBeans IDE - just look for the Built on NetBeans sign!
  • NetBeans t-shirts! - Answer a short questionaire for a free NetBeans t-shirt! (while supplies last).
  • Birds of a feather sessions (BOFs) - These less formal meetings/presentations/discussions are where you can learn and meet other users and developers of the NetBeans IDE and Platform (listed below).
  • Technical sessions - These are in-depth presentations on some technical aspect relating to NetBeans (listed below).
If you have an interesting plug-in or a module that you would like us to show at JavaONE in NetBeans, let us know.
