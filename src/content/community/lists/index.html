NetBeans Mailing Lists and Forums

Looking for NetBeans IDE support, community news, or release announcements? You can communicate with the community via mailing lists, online forums, or news groups. The forums and news groups are synchronized with the mailing lists. This means a comment posted on a forum will appear on the corresponding mailing list, and vice versa.

  2. NetBeans Forums

    Official NetBeans Forums

    The official forums.netbeans.org are synchronized with the most widely-used NetBeans Mailing Lists.

    Note: Your netbeans.org password will not work, please sign up here to post on forum.netbeans.org.


    External NetBeans Forums on Nabble.com

    Nabble's NetBeans forums are sync'ed with our mailing lists. Register with Nabble here. Messages posted via Nabble will be moderated by us unless you contact the list owner and request to be added to the list of non-subscribers allowed to post.

  3. Usenet / NNTP

    Point your newsreader at news.gmane.org and search for "netbeans" groups. To post to the lists via NNTP, reply to the GMane authorisation request. Messages posted via NNTP will be moderated by us unless you contact the list owner and request to be added to the list of non-subscribers allowed to post.

List and Forum Etiquette

  • Search the web for an answer first.

  • Tell us what your goal was, which steps you tried, and what unexpected behaviour (errors etc) you got.

  • Don't post the same message to more than one list.

  • Trim the original text in your reply. Trim code samples.

  • Please treat the list like people. :-)
