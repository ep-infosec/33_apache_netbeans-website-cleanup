NetBeans Community Chats


Chat sessions are a great way to talk in real time directly to people from all levels of the NetBeans community. Upcoming moderated chats will always be announced in the news on the Community portal a week or 2 before the event, and on this page.

We are now using the built-in Developer Collaboration module as client for out chat sessions. If you don't have it set up yet, take a look at this cool Developer Collaboration step-by-step tutorial. For questions or comments, please contact our chat coordinator Jiri.

Tip: Make sure to check the start time on the World Clock!
