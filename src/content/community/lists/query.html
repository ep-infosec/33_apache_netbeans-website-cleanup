List Subscription Query


If you have more than one email address, and mail forwarded between addresses, it's possible to lose track of which address is subscribed to which list. This can be a problem when you're trying to unsubscribe or change addresses.

This form allows you to check which top-level netbeans.org mailing lists you are subscribed to. Just enter your email address below, and the results - including detailed unsubscription info - will be mailed to you.
