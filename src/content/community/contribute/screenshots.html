Submit an Application Screenshot!

One way you can help the NetBeans community is to contribute screenshots of your application built on the NetBeans platform. Check out the existing platform screenshots page to see what others have contributed.

If you've come here looking for screenshots of features of the NetBeans IDE, look at the features pages.

Submission Guidelines for Application Screenshots

Here are some quick guidelines for submissions:

  • Provide a short description of the application and where to get more information;

  • Send a couple of different shots, up to 3 or 4, showing variations of windows, workspaces, configurations, etc;

  • Try to include a couple of different windows in each screenshot, showing some of the more interesting aspects of your application;

  • Include a brief description of your environment - OS, NetBeans version, anything else relevant;

  • Include a brief description of what is visible in the image, what is happening (eg "import button for large images");

  • Shots should be in as high resoloution and colour depth as you can (within reason), and in any standard image format - we can always scale down and reduce colour depth for the website;

  • If your shots are accepted and published, you will be credited by name (not email), unless you specifically ask not to be;

  • Send your screenshots to webmaster at netbeans dot org;

Thanks for contributing to netbeans.org!
