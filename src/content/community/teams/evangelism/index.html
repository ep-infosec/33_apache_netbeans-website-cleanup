NetBeans Evangelism & Event Resources Kit


Every NetBeans user is an evangelist! By sharing your knowledge and enthusiasm about NetBeans technology to fellow developers, students, clients and others, you help to grow and strengthen the community.

This page offers a set of resources--slides, presentations, and links--that can be used to provide information to others about NetBeans technology at university events, user group meetings, developer conferences, company presentations and more.
