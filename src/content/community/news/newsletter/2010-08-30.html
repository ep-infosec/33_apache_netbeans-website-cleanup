Project News

NetBeans Sessions at JavaOne

The NetBeans team and the broader NetBeans community are delivering a wide variety of presentations at JavaOne. Read the whole list here!

NetBeans UML Moves to Community

Equinox Better with NetBeans Platform

From the Netbinox project comes more news about how and why Equinox works better when combined with the NetBeans Platform.

Military Software Development on the NetBeans Platform
