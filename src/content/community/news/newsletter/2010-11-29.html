NetBeans 7.0 Community Translations Starting!

Community translations for NetBeans 7.0 (Platform, JavaSE modules and many more) are kicking off! Language teams of various sizes are contributing localization in Catalan and Galician, as well as in German, French, Spanish, Turkish, Korean, Swedish, Italian, Dutch, Polish, Romanian, Indonesian or Sinhalese, and other languages.

To be a part of the translation community team, visit the NetBeans Localization Project pages or find out more on NetBeans.org.
