Calendar

17 - 18 April -- NetBeans Day Greece

NetBeans Day Greece was an enjoyable and informative event. Sessions and hands on workshops were held near Athens, to be repeated there soon! Read all about it here, in a blog entry by Mark Stephens from IDR Solutions, who was one of the speakers.

29 May -- NetBeans Day UK

Not many tickets are left for NetBeans Day UK. Join the NetBeans Community at the first NetBeans event to be held in the UK. Speakers will include Ben Evans from the London JUG, Ian Utting from BlueJ, Mark Stepens from IDR Solutions, and Geertjan Wielenga from NetBeans. This is a free event.

22 June -- NetBeans Day Brazil

Right before JavaOne Brazil, NetBeans Day Brazil will take place. Join the NetBeans Community at this free event and learn about the latest NetBeans features while networking with developers from all over the world.
