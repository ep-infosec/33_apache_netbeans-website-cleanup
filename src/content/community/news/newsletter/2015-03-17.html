Not long ago, in December 2014, the Oracle office in Munich, Germany, was used for the first time to host a NetBeans Day. It was a big success. At that event, we immediately decided we wanted to have another day just like it and soon! Monday, 16 March 2015, the second NetBeans Day was held at the Oracle office in Munich.
