NetBeans Newsletters

There is a lot happening at netbeans.org. To help you stay on top of the latest developments, a team of volunteer editors put together a weekly newsletter of articles, tutorials, events and other NetBeans-related content. If you'd like to help edit or contribute to the newsletter, please contact us! If you're a newsletter editor, check out the Newsletter writing guidelines.

How do I subscribe ?

Easy! Just send a blank message to the subscribe address, and reply to the automated confirmation request.

Submit News

If you would like to submit some news or something of interest for inclusion in either newsletter, please mail nbnews_contrib@netbeans.org. Remember you can also submit news anytime for the regular website news listings.
