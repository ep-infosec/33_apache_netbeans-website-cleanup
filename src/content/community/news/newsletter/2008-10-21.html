Project News

NetBeans 6.5 Release Candidate Now Available

NetBeans.org is proud to announce the availability of NetBeans IDE 6.5 Release Candidate! The focus of NetBeans IDE 6.5 is simplified and rapid development of web, enterprise, desktop, and mobile applications with PHP, JavaScript, Java, C/C++ , Ruby, and Groovy. The Release Candidate improves on the support offered in 6.5 Beta. Learn more about the release, get tutorials, and download the release today!

Show Your NetBeans Spirit - Enter the NetBeans 10th Birthday Decathlon!

The NetBeans 10th Birthday Bash is on! Be a part of the celebration by taking part in our Birthday Decathlon. You don't need to be an athlete, just enthusiastic about the NetBeans project and community! Watch a NetBeans webinar, review a plugin, submit a video greeting, complete a survey, join our Facebook group and more. Complete any number of tasks that add up to 50 points and score a cool limited edition birthday t-shirt!


Video: A Birthday Greeting from James Gosling!

What better way to kick off our 10th birthday bash than with a retrospective and birthday greeting from the Godfather of Java, James Gosling! Visit the NetBeans 10th Birthday page throughout the week for more unique content celebrating ten years of the NetBeans community!

NetBeans IDE at the International PHP Conference (Oct. 27-31)

Meet the NetBeans team at the 2008 International PHP Conference in Mainz, Germany. NetBeans will host a session describing PHP support in the latest release of the NetBeans IDE, as well as JavaScript/Ajax and database tools available in the IDE. Also, visit the Sun booth and get information about MySQL and OpenSolaris.
