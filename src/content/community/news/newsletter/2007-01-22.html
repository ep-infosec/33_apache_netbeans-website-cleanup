Congratulations to Adam Bien, Vincent Brabant, Aristides Villarreal Bravo, Wade Chandler, Fabrizio Giudici, Masoud Kalali, Jörg Plewe, Vinicius Senger, Edgar Silva, David Strupl, and Ibon Urrutia. They are the 11 charter members of the newly launched NetBeans Dream Team. The Dream Team is a community-driven group of highly skilled NetBeans users who have a proven record of enthusiasm for and participation in NetBeans events.
