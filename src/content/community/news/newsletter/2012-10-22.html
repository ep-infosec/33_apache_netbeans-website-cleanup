Project News

NetBeans IDE 7.2.1 Released

NetBeans IDE 7.2.1 is an update to NetBeans IDE 7.2 and includes support for JavaME 3.2 SDK, JavaFX 2.2 SDK, and integration of recent patches.

Plugin: phpcsmd

This plugin reports code measurements generated by phpcs, phpmd, phpcpd and pdepend as Tasks and Annotations.
