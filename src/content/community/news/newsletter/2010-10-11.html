Project News

NetBeans 6.10 Changes to NetBeans 7.0

The NetBeans team is renaming the next release, NetBeans 6.10, to NetBeans 7.0. The version change aligns NetBeans with plans for the Java platform; thus NetBeans 7.0 will provide support for JDK 7 language features and JDK 7 Beta. Learn more about this change to the NetBeans release roadmap.

New Plug-in: Confirm On Exit

"confirm.exit" is a new plug-in that solves this user pet peeve. Created by Anuradha Gunasekara, the plug-in adds the option of confirming before quitting your work. But you can also choose in the settings to "Always exit without prompt".
