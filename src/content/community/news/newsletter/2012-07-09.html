Project News

NetBeans IDE 7.2 Community Acceptance Survey

Is NetBeans IDE 7.2 ready for full release? Your feedback is needed. The deadline has been extended to Wednesday July 11th, close of business in last time zone. What are your thoughts on 7.2's quality, performance, and improvements? Give us your candid feedback in the 7.2 community acceptance survey.

Articles

The Enterprise Side of JavaFX: Part Two

NetBeans Dream Team member Adam Bien continues his interesting series of articles where he integrates JavaFX into a Java EE application!

Statistical Computation Tool on NetBeans

Artur Kustra introduces a new tool he's created for experiments in mechanics. The tool uses the same infrastructure as NetBeans IDE.

Networked Bus System Control System on NetBeans

Telemotive AG develops complex networked bus systems for control devices, comparable to network routers, and the related management applications are developed on NetBeans.

Java Embedded @ JavaOne 2012 Call for Papers

At Java Embedded @ JavaOne, C-level executives, architects, business leaders, and decision makers will gather to learn how Java Embedded technologies and solutions offer compelling value. The conference Call for Papers–open through July 18, 2012–is seeking business-focused content that is unique, valuable, and relevant.

Moving the Quick Search Box to the Menu Bar

Hermien Pellissier, an active member of the NetCAT community, shows you how to move the Quick Search Box into the menu bar of the IDE or another NetBeans Platform application.

The Java hint infrastructure is pluggable. Learn how to search for your own code patterns and fix them via the "Inspect & Transform" tool in the IDE.
