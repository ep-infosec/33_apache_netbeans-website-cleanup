What Say You? NetBeans Governance Board Term Extension

The current NetBeans Governance Board has submitted a proposal to extend the board term from six months to one year for itself and for future board cycles. The longer term will allow future boards more time to carry out and complete initiatives. Follow the discussion or give your opinion on the NB Discuss mailing list. (NB.org login required)
