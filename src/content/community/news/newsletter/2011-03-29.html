Issue # 480 - Mar 29, 2011

Project News

Vote for the 18th NetBeans Governance Board!

Who are the Governance Board nominees and how are they involved in the NetBeans Community? Read their profiles and vote your choice! The voting period runs from Monday, March 28 through Monday, April 11. The results and the new board will be announced Tuesdday, April 12.

NetBeans IDE 7.0 Release Candidate 1 Available

The NetBeans Team is pleased to announce the first release candidate build of NetBeans IDE 7.0. Download the build today and give us feedback on the NetBeans mailing lists & forums, write us via Twitter, or file a report. The final release of NetBeans 7.0 is planned for April.
