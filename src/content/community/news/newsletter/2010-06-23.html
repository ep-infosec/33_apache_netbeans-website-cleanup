Fedora 13 Supports NetBeans IDE

Fedora 13, the newest release of the free Red Hat-sponsored Linux distribution, includes NetBeans software. The NetBeans Platform and the Java SE development module of NetBeans IDE 6.8 can be installed on Fedora 13 by clicking on the "NetBeans package" in Fedora's "Add/Remove Software" functionality. All other features/modules of NetBeans 6.8 are available from the NetBeans Update Centers.
