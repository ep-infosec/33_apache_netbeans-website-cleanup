Issue # 467 - Dec 20, 2010

Project News

Patch3 for NetBeans IDE 6.9.1 Now Available

The NetBeans team has released a new patch, which is an update to NetBeans IDE 6.9.1. The patch includes bug fixes in modules for C/C++, IDE Platform, Java, and PHP. Get more information about NetBeans IDE 6.9 Patches. To obtain the fixes, the NetBeans IDE must be installed and running. You can download the fixes through the IDE's Plugin Manager.

Plugin: History Clipboard

Learn about a module that implements a clipboard that keeps track of copied items so they can be pasted later.

GlassFish Support for NetBeans IDE 7.0

Vince Kraemer from the GlassFish team reports on the latest developments on the GlassFish plugin for NetBeans IDE 7.0. And he's looking for your comments and ideas!
