Issue # 510 - Oct 25, 2011

Project News

NetBeans Poll: How do you contribute to NetBeans?

There are many different ways in which you can become involved in the NetBeans project. Here's the list! Please indicate where your involvement lies in the latest NetBeans community poll.

NetBeans Podcast 57: Ernest Lotter, Institute of Mine Seismology

Tune in for chats about the development status of NetBeans 7.1 and a recap of NetBeans news from JavaOne 2011, as well as an interview about how the NetBeans Platform is used for earthquake monitoring by the mining industry.

Plugin: SerialVersionUID Generator for NetBeans 7.0.1

NetBeans Dream Team member John Yeary reports on Michal Hlaváč's plugin for generating serial version data in your Serializable classes. Try it out and give him feedback!

Articles

Mine Seismology on the NetBeans Platform

Data from mines around the world is sent to the Institute of Mine Seismology in Stellenbosch, South Africa. Several applications based on NetBeans analyze the data and help ensure mine safety.

NetBeans @ JavaOne Report

Geertjan presents a short report on the state of NetBeans at JavaOne. Were you at JavaOne too? Why not blog about your NetBeans experiences there?

Tutorials

Getting Started with FXML

Learn how to create a JavaFX FXML application using NetBeans IDE 7.1 Beta. This guide provides step-by-step instructions for using the FXML template to create the user interface for a login application.
